Charlotte

The Queen City

Already being dubbed the banking capital of America, the Queen City has much more to offer than meets the eyes. Along with the name, rich historical ties are seen throughout the city, whether it be through the architecture or by visiting a museum. You can take a trip to the boardwalk and go paddle boating, watch the Panthers play, or even just walk around downtown. Explore our thriving downtown with seemingly endless choices of bars and restaurants that are waiting for you.
