To Do: Activities for all!

Paddle Boating at Boardwalk

Enjoy a warm sunny day by taking a walk around the boardwalk, maybe grabbing a bite from the multiple dessert areas and restaurants around you while you walk. Then head over to the paddle boating area and have a blast! You can also buy duck feed before you hop on, and feed the numerous ducks in the area! This is a fun activity for all ages. Pricing per boat is $15 for every 30 minutes.

The Charlotte Museum of History

Explore Charlotte's history through engaging exhibits, events, and tours at The Charlotte Museum of History. The oldest house in mecklenburg county is on the land of this museum! This is a family friendly experience as well. You can explore the entire museum by yourself, or take a guided tour! Admission starts at $10 for adults, $7 for seniors and children 6-17, and free for kids under the age of 6.

Charlotte Center City Carriage Tours

Tour Center City and neighboring historic districts in a horse-drawn carriage. Many free events happen in downtown, ranging from art shows, all the way to food festivals. Prices for the carriage tour depend on how long of a tour you take. A 20 min tour is $12 per person, 40 min tour is $24 per person, and the one hour tour is $45 per person.

Panthers

Charlotte is the home of the Panthers! The stadium is located in the heart of downtown, and we are not used to winning. This activity is catered towards anyone that enjoys sports, but it is still a family friendly activity. Prices for this activity vary based on what other team we are playing, however, an average cost of ticket is around $55 per person.
